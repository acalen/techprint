About Us

Techprint, Inc. is a contract plastics and metal finishing company located in Elk Grove Village, Illinois, founded by the Roth family in 1984. Since our inception, we have become an industry leader in sublimation printing on high temperature thermoplastics and in epoxy printing. Through our dedication to quality, we have been able to blend our expert knowledge with our engineering standards to reduce the time needed for production.

We have built our business on these qualities and it is our goal to continue to use these qualities to develop new and lasting business relationships. Please give us a try and contact us today so that we can show you that we are the only part finisher you will need!
