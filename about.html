About Us

Techprint, Inc. was founded in 1984 by the Roth family. We started business with a focus on the keyboard and automotive markets. Since our beginning, we have grown from our initial facilities into the current 11,650 square foot facility we now occupy in Elk Grove Village, Illinois. Since our inception, we have developed into a leader in sublimation printing on high temperature thermoplastics and in epoxy printing on almost any part and surface all whilst maintaining our dedication to quick turn around at the highest quality.

We have built our business on these qualities and as a result we have had many long term satisfied customers. Our goal is to develop new and lasting business relationships through quality, engineering, customer service, and pricing.

We have succeeded by tackling some of the most complex jobs. Try us out to see that Techprint, Inc. is the only part finisher you will need. Please visit our gallery to see examples of our work.
