Custom Key Tops

Techprint, Inc. can source your key top requirements for almost any keyboard manufacturer. We can produce the key top, custom print your legends, package your orders, and stock to your specifications.

We utilize a wet process dye sublimation printing method on high temperature thermoplastics that actually dyes the image into the key top. This process is available in black, red, and green. Other colors are available but require additional research. We can take your engineered drawing for a new key and source it in the U.S.A.

Key Benefits

  • Single source for all keytop needs
  • Sourcing for keytops by most manufacturers
  • Fully customizable legends and cap cords

Capabilities

We have the capability to produce your keytop from nearly any manufacurer in a variety of different colors for the assembly of a unique keyboard layout. We can also kit and inventory your keytops as your require.

Our design department will work with you to develop custom designs for your legend. Our engineering department will work with you to provide thermally permanent legends and develop a custom solution to imprint your legends.
